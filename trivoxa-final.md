## **HOME PAGE** 

## **Objective** 

The Home page should create a powerful first impression, introduce Trivoxa Group, establish trust, and encourage visitors to explore the company's different business areas. It should act as the gateway to the entire website rather than containing all information itself. 

## **1. Hero Section** 

## **Objective** 

The Hero section is the first thing visitors experience. Its purpose is to immediately communicate Trivoxa's identity, ambition, and professionalism while creating a memorable visual experience. 

## **Content** 

- Trivoxa Group logo 

- Navigation bar 

- Main headline 

- Supporting description 

- Primary CTA (Request a Quote) 

- Secondary CTA (Explore Businesses) 

## **Animation** 

Instead of a static image, display the Trivoxa Eagle built entirely from thousands of glowing particles. 

The eagle should have subtle movement such as: 

- Particle shimmer 

- Slight breathing motion 

- Gentle particle drift 

- Mouse interaction 

As the visitor begins scrolling, the eagle should dissolve into particles and smoothly transition into the next section. 

## **Purpose** 

This section should create curiosity, establish premium branding, and encourage the user to continue scrolling. 

## **2. About Preview** 

## **Objective** 

Provide a concise introduction to Trivoxa without overwhelming the visitor. 

This section should answer: 

- Who is Trivoxa? 

- What does Trivoxa do? 

- Why was Trivoxa created? 

## **Content** 

- Short company introduction 

- Vision summary 

- Mission summary 

- Brief company philosophy 

- "Learn More" button leading to the Group page 

## **Animation** 

Particles from the Hero section gradually reorganize into a connected network behind the content. 

The background remains alive with subtle movement. 

## **Purpose** 

Build trust while encouraging visitors to explore the full Group page. 

## **3. Businesses Preview**

## **Purpose** 

Help visitors quickly understand what Trivoxa offers and direct them to the Businesses page.
