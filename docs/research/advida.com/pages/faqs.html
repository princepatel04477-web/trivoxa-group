    What Services Does Advida Provide?

    A: Advida provides digital advertising and content development to brands and services. Our core service is new customer acquisition. We use paid media campaigns to promote your product or service to generate new customers at profitable rates. We track campaign performance in real-time, weighing your revenues against advertising costs to ensure new customers are acquired in a profitable manner.

    What Is Performance Marketing?

    A: Performance marketing is advertising where the client pays for results achieved. Unlike traditional marketing, performance marketing is specifically used to drive actions and track and measure those actions in real-time to optimize profitability or ROAS.

    What Is A Paid Advertising Agency?

    A: A paid advertising agency is a company that specializes in driving new customers to your product or service using paid media. Success is measured by the following metrics to ensure new customers are acquired at a profitable rate:

     

    Return on ad spend (ROAS)
    Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)
    Cost Per Lead (CPL)
    Cost Per Thousand Impressions (CPM)
    Cost Per Click (CPC)
    Effective Cost Per Action (eCPA)
    Conversion Rate (CR)

    Click Through Rate (CTR)

    What Paid Marketing Services Do You Use?

    A: Our team can implement a wide variety of paid media strategies to drive new customers to your brand:

     

    Determining high-intent keywords based on your product or service that create high ROAS search campaigns.
    Developing custom static image and video creatives that can be advertised via social media platforms, Google Ads, YouTube, and through native advertising.
    Utilizing direct response copywriting to increase conversion rates on your website, landing pages, and creatives.
    Running audience based campaigns leveraging existing data based on your customer avatars.

    A: The onboarding process is fast. When you start a project you’ll be assigned a senior media buyer that will provide you with a checklist of items for onboarding.
